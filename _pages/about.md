Hi, I am **Guangli Hu** (胡广理), an undergraduate student at the [School of Mathematical Sciences](https://math.ustc.edu.cn/), University of Science and Technology of China.
Before USTC, I studied at Harbin No. 3 High School, Nangang Campus, and was a member of the Heilongjiang provincial mathematics team in high school.

My current academic interests are centered around **3D vision (3DV)** and **computer graphics (CG)**.
I have been fortunate to stay in close contact with Prof. [Juyong Zhang](https://staff.ustc.edu.cn/~juyong/), whose work and guidance have strongly shaped my taste in geometry, graphics, and visual computing.

Outside research and coursework, I spend a lot of time in sports, especially **track and field** and **basketball**.
I like the way athletic training keeps life concrete: progress is measured honestly, and small improvements accumulate.
